Professional Summary

Ph.D. candidate in Geography and Geographic Information Science at UIUC. Research interests include geospatial AI, remote sensing, hydrologic feature extraction, and cyberGIS. Aims to build spatially transferable deep learning models for environmental sustainability and academic contributions.

  • B.Eng. in Computer Engineering, Chulalongkorn University, Thailand, 2011

Research Interests

  • Geospatial Artificial Intelligence (GeoAI)
    AI methods tailored for spatial data, including image segmentation and representation learning.
  • Hydrologic Mapping & Watershed Analysis
    Automated delineation of streams and catchments using high-resolution elevation and remote sensing data.
  • Remote Sensing & Earth Observation
    Extracting land and water features from satellite and aerial imagery through supervised and unsupervised learning.
  • Deep Learning and Transfer Learning
    Leveraging pretrained models for geospatial tasks to improve generalization and reduce training costs.
  • CyberGIS and High-Performance Computing
    Scaling spatial data science workflows with CyberGIS and parallel computing environments.

Advisor: Dr. Shaowen Wang
